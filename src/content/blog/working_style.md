
---
title: "Working Style"
date: 2026-03-03
---

I value environments that are technically rigorous and genuinely human at the same time.

Respect, tolerance and mutual trust are not secondary qualities — they are structural properties of strong teams. I prefer cultures where people can disagree constructively, communicate directly, and still keep perspective and humor. Dry humor included.

Professionalism, in my view, is not coldness. It is clarity combined with responsibility. The best teams I have worked in were demanding — but also human.

I work well in settings where ownership is real, expectations are explicit, and feedback is honest. Autonomy and accountability go hand in hand.
